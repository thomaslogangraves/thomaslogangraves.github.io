Why Go Traveling?

Most people could stay at home and save money. It's also generally safer to stay at home than to travel. So why do it? I would describe it an necessary part of becoming an adult. Now, it's important to mention when I am talking about travel I don't mean anything other than leaving your hometown area to explore somewhere new. By this definition most people have gone traveling to some extent or another. That is the wonderful thing about traveling, it can be a short or a long journey.

Where Do You Want to Go?

While it is a simple question it is also the most important. You can't go everywhere all at once so it is important to really figure out what are your top spots to visit. Take a look below to help figure out where you want to travel.

Criteria for Where to Travel

  • What's the weather like?
  • Why am I going?
  • What do I want to do there?
  • How far is it?
  • How much time do I have?
