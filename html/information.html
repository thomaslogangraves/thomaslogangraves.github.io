Europe

Europe has a wide variety of landscapes mixed with well preserved aspects of civilizations past and present. Traveling here is simplified by the majority of countries operating on the Euro and being part of the European Union. Look below at the table to see a table of a few countries and their languages and currencies.



Country Language Currency
France French Euro
Germany German Euro
Poland Polish Zloty
England English Pound
Romania Romanian Romanian Leu

South America

South America is rich in jungles, mountains and dry lands. While here you can either plunge into the mysterious Amazon or Gaze upon the miraged salt flats. South America is still untamed in many areas making it a great land of adventure.
